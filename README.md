# COS511 FA1 Assignment — COS511-FA-ASSIGNMENT Repo

Welcome to the **COS511 FA1 Assignment** repository.  
This repo is your central place to submit your **FA1 video and summary** for evaluation.

---

## **Introduction**

In this assignment, you will explore key concepts related to IT support within a medium-sized law firm, focusing on:

- Identifying and explaining key computer components
- Demonstrating the installation process of these components
- Understanding how components work together in a system

By completing this assignment, you will gain practical insights into IT systems critical for maintaining smooth business operations.

---

## **Assignment Tasks**

1. **Record your video** (max 10 minutes) showing:  
   - Identification and explanation of each key computer component.  
   - Demonstration of the installation process of each component.  
   - Clear close-up views and verbal explanations.

2. **Upload your video to YouTube as “Unlisted”** (recommended):
   - Go to YouTube → Upload video → Visibility → Unlisted.  
   - Copy the **video link (URL)**.

3. **Submit your video link and summary via GitHub Issues**:
   - Go to the **Issues tab** in this repo.  
   - Click **New Issue → FA1 Submission template** (pre-filled).  
   - Fill in your **video link** and **summary**.

**Summary must include:**
- Overview of the components you identified and assembled  
- Insights on how the components work together in a computer system  
- Key learning from completing this task

> ✅ **Important:** Do **not upload video files** to GitHub. Only provide the link to your YouTube video.

---

## **Submission Instructions (Step-by-Step)**

1. Record your video (≤10 min).  
2. Upload to **YouTube Unlisted** and copy the link.  
3. Open the **COS511-FA-ASSIGNMENT** repo → **Issues → New Issue**.  
4. Select the **FA1 Submission template**.  
5. Paste your **YouTube link** in the “Video link” field.  
6. Fill in your **Summary**.  
7. Submit the issue before the **submission deadline**.

You can access the template directly: [Create New Submission Issue](https://github.com/CTU-IT-SPACE/COS-FA-ASSIGNMENT/issues/new)
